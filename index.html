Dose Rate Calculator – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 05 September 2025

Overview

Dose Rate Calculator is an offline Android application. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Collection

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No analytics, ads, crash reporting, or tracking are used.
  • The app does not connect to the Internet.

Permissions

The app requests no runtime permissions. It does not access contacts, location, photos, microphone, or camera.

Data Handling

All calculations occur locally on the device. No data leaves the device.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for professional/educational use. It does not target children.

Security

The app is code-signed and distributed via the Google Play Store. There are no network connections and no remote data stores.
